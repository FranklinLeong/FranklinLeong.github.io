Education

Bachelor of Science with Hons (Life Sciences), NUS, Singapore

I am currently in my final year, majoring in Life Sciences at National University of Singapore. I am also concurrently taking up a minor in Artificial Intelligence. I am fortunate to have garnered multiple research experiences through my undergraduate life. My next stage of life would be to embark on a Ph.D programme to pursue neuroscience and artificial intelligence.

Student Exchange Programme (Life Sciences Engineering), EPFL, Switzerland

During the second semester of my third year as an undergraduate student, I had the honour of going to EPFL, Switzerland for student exchange programme. Over there, I took two computational neuroscience modules and one neuroscience module which reinforced my love for neuroscience. I also had the chance to work with Dr. Eduardo for a semester long project which continued even after I returning to Singapore.

Scholarships and Awards

I am honoured to be a recipient of the A*STAR Undergraduate Scholarship, which was awarded to young aspiring scientific talent pursuing their passion in science. This allowed me to focus on my studies and joined different laboratories, enriching my view on neuroscience researchs. I was also on the Dean's list for AY 18/19, which encouraged me to work even harder for my academics.
