Research Interests

Neuroscience

My first encounter with neuroscience was when I read ‘Tales from Both Sides of the Brain: A Life in Neuroscience’ by Michael S. Gazzaniga. Learning how the left and right brain can harbour different thoughts was so mind blowing that kindled my love for neuroscience ever since. Neuroscience is a truly interdisciplinary field where the brightest mind from biology, psychology, physics, mathematics, and computer science come together to solve the mysteries that shrouds the brain.

Artificial Intelligience

As I get deeper into neuroscience research, I began to uncover my love for numbers and quantitative analysis. This motivated my to pursue a minor in Artificial inteligience and I love how AI can be applied in so many different scenario. In the 21st century, AI had became so ingrained in our life whether we realize it or not. I feel that AI and neuroscience greatly complement each other and aims to integrate these two field.

Brain Machine Interface

One particular area that I hope to apply the marriage between AI and neuroscience is in the field of Brain-Machine Interface. Through the electrodes that are inserted into the brain, we are able to receive many data. However, I believe that AI is the key to convert those data into information that we can understand and utilize. I hope to create a neuroprosthesis that an amputee would be able to control using the mind without feeling the difference from an biological arm. This would also require extensive knowledge in encoding and feeding the infomation to the brain, allowing them to feel what the neuroprosthesis is touching.
